Creamy Chocolate Fudge

Description

Indulge in the rich and decadent flavors of our creamy vegan chocolate fudge, a delightful treat that promises to satisfy your sweet tooth while catering to a plant-based lifestyle. This fudge boasts a smooth, velvety texture, achieved through the perfect blend of coconut milk and dairy-free chocolate, creating a luscious melt-in-your-mouth experience. With just a hint of vanilla and a pinch of sea salt to enhance the depth of flavors, each bite offers a harmonious balance of sweetness and richness, making it an irresistible dessert for chocolate lovers everywhere.

Not only is this fudge a delicious treat, but it's also incredibly easy to make, requiring just a handful of simple ingredients and minimal preparation time. By using coconut milk as the base, this recipe ensures a creamy consistency without the need for dairy, while the addition of maple syrup provides a natural sweetness that complements the rich chocolate. Perfect for sharing at gatherings or enjoying as a personal indulgence, this vegan chocolate fudge is a versatile and satisfying dessert that will leave everyone craving more. Whether you're a seasoned vegan or simply looking to explore new dessert options, this recipe is sure to become a favorite in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (14 oz) full-fat coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups dairy-free chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

Steps

  1. Line an 8x8 inch baking dish with parchment paper, leaving some overhang on the sides for easy removal.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the full-fat coconut milk and maple syrup. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture starts to simmer.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and add the dairy-free chocolate chips. Stir continuously until the chocolate is fully melted and the mixture is smooth.
  4. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the vanilla extract and sea salt until well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish, spreading it evenly with a spatula.
  6. Refrigerate the fudge for at least 2 hours, or until it is firm to the touch.
  7. Once the fudge is set, use the parchment paper overhang to lift it out of the baking dish. Cut into small squares and enjoy!
  8. Store any leftover fudge in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to one week.
